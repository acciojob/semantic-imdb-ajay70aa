Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters the dreams of others to steal their secrets. Tasked with planting an idea instead of stealing one, Cobb must navigate complex dream layers while confronting his own haunting past.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, and Tom Hardy as Eames. Each actor delivers a powerful performance that brings the dream-heist narrative to life.
